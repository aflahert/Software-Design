About Poli-Kicks

Poli-Kicks is a debating platform and political Kick-starter. Poli-kicks looks to create a platform through which individuals interested in getting involved in local and national politics can build a public profile online. The process would involve answering questions outlining their beliefs, defending those beliefs in debate and discussion, and hopefully over time connecting with other like minded individuals throught the site to form real world grass roots political organisations and parties. All this information is stored and freely available to anyone who visits the site, so as to allow for more transparency as people make decisions about political issues and figures. The site also facilitates a crowdfunding tool. As groups form and wish to finance themselves they may utilise the tool, again with an interest in increasing transparency in the funding streams of political organisations.
